<p><p>The cytotoxicity assay market consists of various types of kits designed to assess cell viability and toxicity. Cytotoxicity assay-based kits include colorimetric kits, which utilize absorbance measurements to evaluate cell health, and fluorometric kits, employing fluorescence detection for sensitivity. ELISA kits facilitate quantification of soluble cytotoxic factors. Crystal violet kits are used for assessing cell adherence, while minimal inhibitory concentration kits help determine the lowest drug concentration preventing cell growth. Each type serves specific experimental needs in toxicology and drug development.</p></p>
