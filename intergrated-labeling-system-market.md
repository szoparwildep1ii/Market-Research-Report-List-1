<p><p>The Integrated Labeling System market comprises two main types: adhesive-based labeling and non-adhesive based labeling. Adhesive-based labeling utilizes pressure-sensitive or heat-activated adhesives to bond labels to products, ensuring durability and ease of application. In contrast, non-adhesive based labeling includes methods like direct printing or sleeve labeling, which do not rely on adhesives but instead employ mechanical means to affix labels. Both market types cater to various industries, offering solutions that enhance product visibility and compliance with regulatory requirements.</p></p>
