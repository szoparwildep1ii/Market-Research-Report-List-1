<p><p>The portable gloss meters market is segmented into single angle, dual angles, and multi angles. Single angle gloss meters measure reflectance at one fixed angle, providing a straightforward assessment of surface gloss. Dual angle meters offer measurements at two angles, allowing for a more comprehensive gloss profile that accounts for different surface types. Multi angle gloss meters measure reflectance at multiple angles, enabling a detailed analysis of surface textures and finishes, ideal for applications requiring precise gloss evaluation across various materials.</p></p>
